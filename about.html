About Bloomei & Aura
Every candle carries a little bit of home.

Bloomei & Aura began with a simple belief: the right scent, at the right moment, can change how a whole room feels.

Handpoured, one jar at a time

Bloomei & Aura is a small, independent candle studio built around a simple craft: pouring soy wax candles by hand, in batches small enough that every jar gets real attention before it reaches your home.

What started as a personal love for slow evenings, soft light, and good scent has grown into a brand dedicated to bringing that same feeling into other homes — one candle at a time. We choose every fragrance ourselves, test every batch ourselves, and pack every order ourselves.

We believe a candle should do more than just smell nice. It should mark a moment — the end of a long day, a quiet morning, a gift that says you were thought of. That's the feeling we try to capture in every jar we pour.
